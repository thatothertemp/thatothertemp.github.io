What is TED?

What is TEDxNMIMSShirpur?

Technology, for long, was regarded as a independent field which introduced new innovations. In 1984, Richard Wurman felt a movement wherein technology overtook the way we felt and experienced entertainment and design. He set out to host the first TED(Technology Entertainment Design) conference which oversaw groundbreaking crossovers such Philips and Sony's demo of CD, Apple Macintosh, among others. Subsequently, as the conference blew up, TED biologically broadened it's horizons to include scientists, philosphers; anyone, who had an idea worth sharing.
This was a special event that distributed knowledge first hand, not a journalist's outlook. As TED was always at the cutting edge of technology, it really took advantage of internet's ability to reach a far greater audience. Today, TED boasts of a myraid of conferences held around the world, varying in topics, speakers and audience; they all strive for knowledge from the freshest sources.
TEDx is one of the many initiaves undertaken by TED. It recognizes the limitation of TED to give a platform to everyone and global aspect of the same. Through TEDx, TED appoints curators around the world that are given the authority to host an independent TED like event within guidelines which reenforce the quality TED is known for. TEDx have grown to be appreciated around the world because they are an amalagamation of TED's platform with the ideas of their appointed curator which creates a unique platform of itself.
