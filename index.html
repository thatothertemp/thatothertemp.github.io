TEDxNMIMSShirpur

TED, is an organization that promotes “ideas worth sharing” and, lends its name and support to TEDx, a series of conferences on urban futures held in several cities and organizations around the world.

TED popularized as a metaculture that brought together varied people in varied job roles, all united by a zest of learning first, and sharing. With growth, TED started bringing this annual platform to our local institutions through TEDx, an independently organized TED event that sustains it's core values.

TEDx at NMIMSShirpur is the inception of our curator, Akash Agarwal, who believed in his fellows in sharing the zest for learning as he fosters it too. As a team here, at TEDxNMIMSShirpur, we always strive for ideas that need a platform as a showcase of originality and professionalism.

In 2018, we return with an aggresive team, thought provoking speakers and, an overall delight for your mind.

For 2018, our theme is "Knocking Opportunities".
