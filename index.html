TEDxNMIMSShirpur

TED, is an organization that promotes “ideas worth sharing” and,
lends its name and support to TEDx, a series of conferences
on urban futures held in several cities and organizations around the world.

TED popularized as a metaculture that brought together varied people in
varied job roles, all united by a zest of learning first, and sharing.
With growth, TED started bringing this annual platform to our local institutions
through TEDx, an independently organized TED event that sustains it's core values.

TEDx at NMIMSShirpur is the inception of our curator, Akash Agarwal, who believed
in his fellows in sharing the zest for learning as he fosters it too.
As a team here, at TEDxNMIMSShirpur, we always strive for ideas that need a platform
as a showcase of originality and professionalism.

In 2018, we return with an aggresive team, thought provoking speakers and, an overall delight
for your mind.

For 2018, our theme is "Knocking Opportunities".

Speak at TEDxNMIMSShirpur

What defines you? Your physical self, your work, your values, your experiences or, what you
picked up through your travels? What ever it is, if you believe it is an "idea worth sharing" that
you can deliver on a stage to odd 100 strangers then, you should contact us for further discussion
as a speaker at our upcoming event. Our speaker relations officer will get in touch with you.

Volunteer at TEDxNMIMSShirpur

A little help goes a long way and, sometimes, it feels like we need a ton of help. Your helping hands are a sight for sore eyes.

If you share our passion for TED and affiliated events, and in bring quality ideas to a broader audience
you can join us.

As a volunteer, you will be equipped with tasks pertaining to event management on the event day.
You will be working closely with our team members to assist you along the way.
